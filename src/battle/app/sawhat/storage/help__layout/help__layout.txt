Text (or other elements, but typicaly text) can be cut and displayed as columns. Just wrap text with `[column=2][/column]` or `[column=3][/column]` (yup! old *bbcode style* syntax) for dividing it by 2 or 3 columns (obviously).  
Please keep in mind that this uses css3 rules which means it might not work perfectly on every browser as the time we speak. If it's the case for you please change your browser/device/life to enjoy the future.


Those layout can be upgraded with nice horizontal bar inside your cards by placing 3 or more `*`, `-` or `_` on a line by themselves (example above).
